Dr. Bin Sun is now an Assistant Professor and the Principal Investigator (PI) of the Research Center for Pharmacoinformatics in the College of Pharmacy, Harbin Medical University. He studied in the Huazhong University of Scienece and Technology from 2008-2015 to obtain BSc and Master degrees in Biopharmacy and Medicinal Chemistry, respectively. After that he went to University of Kentucky to pursue a doctoral degree in Computational Biochemisty with Prof. Peter M. Kekenes-Huskey from 2015-2019. After a two-years' postdoc experience in the Stritch School of Medicine, Loyola University Chicago, he obtained the current position. His research interests are to use computational modeling techniqies (mostly molecular dynamics simulations) to "observe" how proteins and nucleic acids behave in a microscopic world, and to reveal the thermodynamics and biophysics underlying these events. While still being facinated by the endless theoretical beauty beneath the biomolecule events, he is also passionate to apply these understandings to better design drugs.

张满杰

张满杰，特聘教授，博士生导师。毕业于东北师范大学，理学博士。研究方向是新型纳米药物递送系统的构建及在生物医学中的应用与研究。目前主要是做无机-聚合物纳米晶功能化介孔材料构筑；基于荧光纳米晶功能化介孔材料的生物纳米技术和生物分析，如早期癌症诊断和治疗，药物储存和缓释，体内生物成像等；生物材料与免疫工程的研究。在Chem. Sci.、Chem. Mater.、Biomaterials、ACS Appl. Mater. Interfaces、Nano-Micro Letters、Acta Biomaterialia、Journal of Materials Science&Technology等国际主流期刊上共发表SCI论文20篇，其中以第一作者及通讯作者发表8篇，一区TOP论文共7篇，影响因子单篇超10的4篇，影响因子总和71.8。目前主持国家自然科学基金青年项目、省科技厅计划项目和哈医大高层次优秀青年人才启动资金等5项科研项目。中国化学会和中国化工学会会员，多次参加国内国际学术会议。担任Chem. Commun.; RSC Adv.; Dyes and Pigments等期刊审稿人
